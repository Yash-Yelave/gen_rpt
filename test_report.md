# Test Report

The EV market is expanding rapidly, with a projected 25% annual growth rate. This presents significant opportunities for investment in charging infrastructure.

## Key Findings
- Battery costs have decreased by 15% year-over-year.
- Government subsidies are expected to be phased out by 2030.

## Recommendations
- Increase investment in solid-state battery research.
- Expand charging network in rural areas to capture emerging markets.
